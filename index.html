campus - life
Your first year of college presents a lot of new experiences: living away from home, meeting new people and managing your own time. Prepare for life on campus by figuring out what to bring, how to handle challenging circumstances and what you can do to manage your workload.Functioning under the aegis of The Delhi Public Society, New Delhi, Delhi Public School, Hisar, situated on NH-9 between two historical cities, Hisar and Hansi, is one of the most prestigious C.B.S.E. Affiliated Schools of the region. Endowed with State Of The Art Infrastructure, Lush Green Premises and World Class Facilities, the school has become a rendezvous of more than 1200 aspiring learners from the vicinity. Irrespective of their color, caste, creed and religious denomination, here they receive the fruits of education and prepare themselves for the University of Life.

ACHIEVEMENTS Proud Moment: Gifting us all a sense of pride at our association with DPS, a young and sturdy Dipsite, Rupak Dhanda has clinched Shining Silver Medal in Boxing Championshiop organised by 65th National School Games Fedaration of India at Saga, Karnal. Impressing the spectactors with his agile and adroit movements and making his opponents bite the dust with his robust punches, the gem added another golden feather in the achievement tiara of our school. The stature of his success got enhanced when Honorable Sports Minister of India, Mr. Kiran Rijiju awarded him with accolades and a certificate of appreciation.The Principal, Mrs. Manju Sudhakar, expressed her elation at this magnanimous success and congratulated the champion, his skilful coach and proud parents.
